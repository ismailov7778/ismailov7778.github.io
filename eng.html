WELCOME

I would like to invite you to the pages of our web-site to learn about our unique school.

Miras International School in Nur-Sultan opened its doors on September 2, 1999. We are often ask, what makes our school different from the others?

Miras is the only school in Nur-Sultan, which is accredited by the Council of International schools (CIS) and the New England Association of Schools and Colleges (NEASC), which is proof of compliance with high international standards.

Miras is the only school in Nur-Sultan, which is authorized to teach the three programmes of the International Baccalaureate (IB). We offer the Primary Years Programme (IBPYP), the Secondary School Programme (IBMYP) and the Diploma Programme (IBDP). Integrated curricula are developed and implemented in the school, and harmoniously combine the requirements of national and international standards.

Miras is the only school, where there is a French Section. The Section Française is accredited by the French Ministry of Education and the language of instruction is French.

At Miras you can find a warm atmosphere of kindness and respect, commitment to universal human values and cooperation, aimed to fully support and develop every child.

Miras School is a place where any success is seen as the success of everyone.

Welcome to Miras International School!

Head of School Yelena Khamitova
