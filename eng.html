TRANSFORMATIONS IN EDUCATION

Dear Miras Community!

The entire world goes through hard times. Despite the complexity of the situation, we are trying to respond to the challenges of the time quickly and accurately. It is important to stay positive, be optimistic and to support our children and their creative ideas. Solidarity and cooperation of all school community members: teachers, students and parents makes it possible to overcome the difficulties of this period of time.

In the short term, the school was reorganized to work within these new conditions of education: educational programmes have been reviewed and adapted; amendments have been made in schedules, consultations and the classes schedule has been changed, additional classes have been added, and the work of all school staff in distance and online teaching and learning have been organized.

With the official status of Microsoft Showcase School, Miras school actively applies Microsoft Office 365 to online and distance learning. The Microsoft Teams app allows teachers and students to work within a holistic digital space, organize on-line classes and provide access to needed educational resources. Transition to distance learning is a complex process and we want to thank parents of our students for their priceless support within these current circumstances.

Despite the fact that Pre-School work has been temporarily suspended, Pre-School teachers of senior groups, continue to maintain contact with families of our students and regularly sends materials to parents to organize the development and leisure activities for children.

All school services continue to work: routine technical and preventive work is underway, preparatory events are in the process for completion of our the current year and in preparation for the next ac academic year.

School life goes on and we look forward to meeting our students in home school!

Sincerely yours,

Head of School Yelena Khamitova
